Privacy Policy — Taskify

Effective Date: September 2025

Taskify is a Chrome extension designed for personal task management. Your privacy is very important to us.

Data Collection & Usage

  • Taskify does not collect, transmit, or sell personal information.
  • All tasks, due dates, reminders, and settings are stored locally in your browser using Chrome’s storage API.
  • No data is ever sent to external servers.

Permissions Explained

  • storage — Save tasks locally in your browser.
  • notifications (optional) — Show reminders for tasks.
  • alarms (optional) — Schedule task reminders.

Third-Party Sharing

Taskify does not share any information with third parties.

Children’s Privacy

Taskify is a general audience extension and is not directed toward children under 13.

Policy Changes

We may update this policy if features change. Updates will be reflected here with a revised effective date.
